TINY SCREEN · ESP32-S3

Flash your display

This flashes the Tiny Screen dashboard firmware to your ESP32-S3 board over USB — no software to install.

Board plugged straight into your ZimaBlade/ZimaBoard instead of this computer? Use the on-device flasher →

Step 1: Flash firmware

  • 1 Plug the ESP32-S3 into this machine via USB-C.
  • 2 Click "Connect & Flash" and pick its serial port.
  • 3 Wait for flashing to finish.

This board uses native USB (no separate bridge chip) — if flashing can't connect, hold the board's BOOT button while clicking Connect & Flash, and release it once you see "Installing".

Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Please use Chrome or Edge. Web Serial is not allowed on this page (must be served over HTTPS or localhost).

Step 2: Apply your settings

Once flashing above has finished, click below to send your chosen pages, cycling, and brightness to the device.

Once configured, run the collector script on your ZimaBlade/ZimaBoard to start streaming live stats.

Requires Chrome, Edge, or another Chromium-based browser with Web Serial support.
