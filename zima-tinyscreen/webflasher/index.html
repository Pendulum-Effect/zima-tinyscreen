TINY SCREEN · ESP32-S3

Flash your display

This flashes the Tiny Screen dashboard firmware directly to your ESP32-S3-Touch-LCD-1.28 over USB — no software to install.

  • 1 Plug the ESP32-S3 into this machine via USB-C.
  • 2 Click "Connect" below and pick its serial port.
  • 3 Wait for flashing to finish, then reboot the board.
Your browser doesn't support Web Serial. Please use Chrome or Edge. Web Serial is not allowed on this page (must be served over HTTPS or localhost).

Once flashed, run the collector script on your ZimaBlade/ZimaBoard to start streaming stats to the display.

Requires Chrome, Edge, or another Chromium-based browser with Web Serial support.
